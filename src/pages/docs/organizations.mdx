# Organizations <Icon name="xpack-lg" />

<EnterpriseFeatureCta
  feature="Multi-organization management"
  description="Create and manage isolated organizations with separate assets, users, and policies in a single JumpServer instance."

## Create an organization
<Steps>
<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="OrganizationSet" /> Organizations**.
</div>

<div>
Click **+ Create**.
</div>

<div>
Type the organization name and description.
</div>

<div>
Click **Submit**.
</div>
</Steps>


## Rename global organization

<Steps>
<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="OrganizationSet" /> Organizations**.
</div>

<div>
Click **Setting**.
</div>

<div>
In the **Global org display** field, type the new name.
</div>

<div>
Click **Confirm**.
</div>
</Steps>
