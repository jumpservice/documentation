# System role <Icon name="xpack-lg" />


## Create a system role
<Steps>
<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="Role" /> Roles**.
</div>

<div>
On the right page, click the **System roles** tab.
</div>

<div>
Click **+ Create**.
</div>

<div>
Type the role name and description.
</div>

<div>
Click **Submit**.
</div>
</Steps>


## Configure system role permissions
<Steps>
<div>
<Nav name="system-settings" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="Role" /> Roles**.
</div>

<div>
On the right page, click the **System roles** tab.
</div>

<div>
Click the role name you want to configure to open the details page.
</div>

<div>
Click the **Basic** tab, and in the "Permission" section on the right side of the page, check or uncheck the corresponding permissions.
</div>

<div>
Click **Update**.
</div>
</Steps>
