## Duplicate a user

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="console" />
</div>

<div>
Navigate to the **Users** page and find the relevant user.
</div>

<div>
In the **Actions** column, click the <Icon name="..."/> icon, then click **Duplicate**.
</div>

<div>
In the **Create user** page, modify the user's attributes, especially the "Name", "Username", and "Email".
</div>

<div>
Click **Submit**.
</div>

</Steps>
