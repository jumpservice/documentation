# Manage roles
This topic describes how to manage System roles ande Organization roles (Org roles).

## System roles
### View system roles details
1. In JumpServer, navigate to Console > Roles > System Roles.
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and click the role name to open the details page.
3. On the `Basic` page (default), you can view `Basic information`and more.

### Set system roles permissions
1. In JumpServer, navigate to **Console > Roles > System roles**.
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and click the role name to open the details page.
3. On the `Basic` page (default), locate the **permissions** card on the right. Then select the required permissions and click the **Update** button above.
<img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/roles/set-system-role-permissions.png" alt="set-system-role-permissions" width="550"/>

### Bind user to system role 
1. In JumpServer, navigate to **Console > Roles > System roles**.
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and click the role name to open the details page.
3. On the `Basic` page (default), click `Authorized Users` then locate the `Members` card on the right, select users to bind to the role (support multiple selection), and click **Add** button.

### Unbind users from system role
1. In JumpServer, navigate to **Console > Roles > System roles**.
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and click the role name to open the details page, select `Authorized Users`.
3. In the users list, locate the relevant role and click the **-** in the `Actions` column to remove the user.

### Edit system role properties and information
1. In JumpServer, navigate to **Console > Roles > System roles**. 
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and click **Edit** in the `Actions` column. Alternatively click **Edit** in the top-right corner of the System Role page.
3. Modify the desired properties or information, then click **Submit**.

### Duplicate a system role
1. In JumpServer, navigate to **Console > Roles > System roles**.
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and select **···** dropdown menu in the `Actions` column then click the **Duplicate** to go to the role creation page.
3. Fill in the desired properties or information. Refer to the [Create roles](/docs/roles/create-roles).
4. Click **Submit** to create the role, or **Save & Continue** to create and add more.

### Delete a system role
1. In JumpServer, navigate to **Console > Roles > System roles**.
2. In the System roles list, locate the relevant role and select **···** dropdown menu in the `Actions` column, then click **Delete**. Alternatively click **Delete** in the top-right corner of the System Role page.
3. Confirm the information to be deleted in the popup, then click **OK**.
