# Manage roles

### View role details
Click on the **role name** to access the role details page.
    <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/roles/role-details.png" alt="role-details" width="750"/>
    * **Basic:** Role basic information.
    * **Permission:** Role Permissions. 
    * **Authorized users:** Users bound to this role.
    * **Activities:** Role activity log.

### Set role permissions
Click on the **role name** to access the role details page. Select permissions and click the **Update** button above.
    <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/roles/set-role-permissions.png" alt="set-role-permissions" width="550"/>

### Bind users role
Click on the **role name** to access the role details page. Click **Authorized Users**, then **Members**, select users to bind to the role, and click **Add** button.
    <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/roles/select-bingd-user.png" alt="select-bingd-user" width="450"/>
    <img src="/images/docs/admin-guides/roles/bind-users-role.png" alt="bind-users-role" width="450"/>
