# Discover account

This topic describes discover account configurations.

## Prerequisites
At least one account must be added to the asset before executing discovery tasks. Automation tasks will prioritize accounts marked as privileged for execution.
Recomanded account privilege requirement are as follows:

<HorizontalTable 
headers={['Asset Type', 'Recomanded account privilege requirement']} 
rows={[
    ["Linux", "Root or sudoers"], 
    ["Windows", "Account in Users group or has access using command 'net user'"], 
    ["Windows Active Directory", "User with 'Read all user information' permission"], 
    ["Unix", "Root or sudoers"], 
    ["Mongodb", "User with 'userAdminAnyDatabase' or 'userAdmin' role"], 
    ["Mysql/MariaDB", "root or have access to 'mysql.user'"], 
    ["Postgresql", "superuser or 'pg_read_all_data' role"], 
    ["Oracle", "DBA or user with 'SELECT_CATALOG_ROLE' privilege"],
    ["SQLServer", "Membership in 'sysadmin' role or have access to sys.dm_exec_sessions and sys.sql_logins"]
]}
/>

For **Minimum account privilege requirement** of each automation template. See [Platform settings](/docs/platform-settings/automation-settings).


## Create an account discovery task

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="pam" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="search" /> Discover accounts**.
</div>

<div>
On the right page, click **Account discovery tasks** tab.
</div>

<div>
Above the table, click **Create**.
</div>

<div>
Type a **Name** for the task.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, choose **Assets nodes** to discover accounts for. All assets under the selected nodes will be included.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, choose **Assets** to discover accounts for.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, enable **Sync to assets**. If enabled, the discovered accounts will be stored in JumpServer.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, enable **Check risk**. If enabled, the discovered accounts will be checked for risk.  
</div>

<div>
Optionally, Choose a **Recipient** for the notification. If enabled, the discovered accounts will be sent to the recipient.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, set **Crontab** to schedule the task.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, set **Interval** to run the task. If both interval and crontab are set, crontab is prioritized.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, disable or enable **Active**, which determines whether the task is active or not.
</div>

<div>
Optionally, type a **Description** for the task.
</div>

<div>
Click **Submit** or **Save & Continue**.
</div>

</Steps>

## View execution history and discovered accounts

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="pam" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="search" /> Discover accounts**.
</div>

<div>
On the right side of the page, switch between the **Execution history** and **Account discovered** tabs.
</div>

<div>
In the **Execution history** tab, you can view the **Log** and **Report** of task execution history in the table.<br/>
In the **Account discovered** tab, you can see the discovered accounts in the table.
</div>

</Steps>
