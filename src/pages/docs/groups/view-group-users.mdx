# View users in the group

You can view all users belonging to a specific group in JumpServer. This helps administrators manage group memberships and ensure that users have the appropriate access and permissions based on their group affiliations.

<Steps>
<div>
<Nav name="console" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, under the USERS section, click **<Icon name="UserGroup" /> Groups**.
</div>

<div>
In the group table, click the name of the group whose users you want to view.
</div>

<div>
On the group details page, click the **Users** tab to see the list of users in the group.
</div>
</Steps>
